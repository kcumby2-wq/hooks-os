# Trainer Blueprint — Skill

How the agent runs the **Coached by Hooks Trainer Blueprint** product — the apex of Margin's trainer funnel. **This is Margin's own product, building Margin's own certified-trainer lineage.** Other apex-tier trainers in the TOJ network (e.g., a future Premier Athletes / Sly Johnson repo) productize and sell *their own* Blueprint under *their own* brand name, with their own pricing, their own qualification gates, and their own network tier names — see `toj-network-brain/shared-standards/apex-tier-defaults.md` for the shared skill structure that each apex trainer's Blueprint follows independently. These are parallel, independent products — not one shared funnel with multiple sellers.

The Trainer Blueprint is what a trainer pays Margin $3,500–5,000 to receive: his personal methodology, the blueprint for building a successful training business, access to the STL network, and the right to operate as an STL Certified Trainer.

**Joshua and Max are graduates of THIS specific Blueprint** (Margin's lineage, via the relationship pathway). The productized version makes the same pathway available to credentialed trainers Margin hasn't personally trained, but is willing to vouch for.

## What the Buyer Actually Receives (the productization)

For $3,500–5,000 one-time:

### Phase 1 — Methodology Transfer (Weeks 1–4)
- **Margin's in-person training methodology** delivered through structured video curriculum + a 2-day in-person immersion in DFW (or at Margin's choice of intensive location).
- **Position-specific (WR primary)** — Margin's exact drill progressions, teaching cues, evaluation framework.
- **The why behind the what** — not just drills but the philosophy that makes the drills work.

### Phase 2 — Business Blueprint (Weeks 5–8)
- **How Margin built a $1M+ training business** — structured walkthrough.
- **Revenue mechanics** — in-person rates, evaluations, cohorts, community, content cadence.
- **Operations blueprint** — what tools, what cadence, what hires, what doesn't matter.
- **Mistakes to avoid** — the specific failure modes Margin has watched other trainers hit.

### Phase 3 — Network Integration (Weeks 9–12)
- **STL Certified Trainer status** awarded on completion.
- **Prospect Edge affiliate access** under the TOJ × OG agreement — evaluation revenue from day one.
- **Cohort hosting rights** — the trainer can host STL-branded cohorts in their region.
- **Revenue share spec** — what trainer keeps vs. what flows back to STL on networked revenue.
- **Joshua + Max introductions** as peer-trainers in the network.

### Phase 4 — Operating Brain Setup (Weeks 13–14)
- **TOJ delivers a `<trainer>-os` operating brain repo** seeded from the master template.
- **GHL sub-account setup** under the TOJ agency.
- **First daily workflow runs** — the trainer's brain wakes up with full business context.

### Lifetime
- **STL community membership** (normally $99/mo) included for life.
- **First-look at Margin's open camps and showcases** — chance to bring athletes into Margin's events.
- **Continued PE affiliate revenue** — no time limit.

## Who Is the Buyer

Not every trainer qualifies. The blueprint is built for credentialed operators. Default qualification gates:

- **Athletic background** — played the sport they want to train, at college level or higher.
- **Existing trainer track record** — minimum 2 years actively training athletes, with a small client roster as proof.
- **Geography fit** — operates in a region not already saturated by an existing STL Certified Trainer (Margin signs off on this).
- **Character check** — references from coaches or fellow trainers. Margin vouches his name to the network; he doesn't vouch lightly.

Trainer Blueprint isn't a course you can buy on impulse. It's an application + qualifying-call gate, then payment, then delivery.

## Content & Outreach for Trainer Blueprint

The Trainer Blueprint product needs its own marketing stream — distinct from STL athlete-facing content and from CBH athlete content.

### The audience
Trainers who follow Margin, attend his events, ask him "how did you build this." Existing follower base contains them; outbound to coaching networks unlocks more.

### Content pillars
1. **What Margin learned building a $1M+ training business** (long-form / podcast / IG carousels)
2. **What a trainer needs to NOT do** (counter-conventional wisdom — contrarian hook per the growth dashboard pattern)
3. **The economics of training** (the math — most trainers don't know what they don't know)
4. **STL Certified Trainer wins** (Joshua's German launch, Max's December event, future certs — proof points)

### Sequences
- **Cold outreach to trainers** (LinkedIn + email) — manual review always. Coach/trainer outreach is high-leverage and goes through the network slowly, not at scale.
- **Inbound nurture** — once a trainer signals interest (form fill, DM, etc.), 5-step email sequence over 14 days surfaces Margin's track record, the Joshua/Max case studies, the Trainer Blueprint structure.

## Hard Rules

- **Margin personally vets every applicant** before acceptance. No exceptions. The credential dies the day a bad trainer wears the STL Certified badge.
- **Acceptance criteria are explicit and consistent** — applicants get rejected on documented criteria, not on vibes. Logged in `data/trainer-blueprint-clients/_rejection-log.md` so the criteria stay enforceable as the network scales.
- **Refund terms are clear** — published before purchase. Default: 14-day full refund window; past that, completed-phase model (refund minus the phases already delivered).
- **Revenue share with Certified Trainers is documented per relationship** — not a default percentage applied universally. Each agreement signed independently.
- **No "watered-down" Trainer Blueprint at lower price.** If demand suggests a lighter product, that becomes a separate STL Affiliate tier (lower commitment, lower deliverable list, no certification badge). The Blueprint stays the apex.

## Calibration

The first 5 Trainer Blueprint buyers calibrate the entire product:
- What did they actually use of the 12 + 8 modules? (Trim what gets ignored.)
- How long did delivery actually take? (Adjust the 14-week timeline.)
- What did they ask for that wasn't included? (Identify expansion deliverables for v2.)
- What was the ROI for them within 12 months? (Build the case studies that sell #6 through #20.)

By Trainer Blueprint #10, this skill is iteratively sharpened and the product is at v2.0. The case-study library (Joshua, Max, + the first 5 new buyers) is the marketing engine for the next wave.
