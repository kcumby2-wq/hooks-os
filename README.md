## What's Distinct About Margin's Repo (vs Joshua's, Max's, or any other client repo)

Three things this repo runs that no other client repo runs:

1. **Three parallel funnels.** The athlete funnel ends at Coached by Hooks personal access. The trainer funnel ends at Trainer Blueprint + Certified Trainer status. The athlete-brand/NIL funnel runs Margin's 36+ signed athletes through TOJ's marketing partnership for brand build and deal flow. Every other client repo runs one or two of these. Margin's repo runs all three in parallel.

2. **The athlete brand & NIL pipeline — a first-of-its-kind capability in the network.** Margin's signed roster (6 NFL + 30+ P4) becomes a category of TOJ engagement that doesn't exist elsewhere yet. This repo is where the pattern is built, tested, and proven. The first season of running it sets the standard for every TOJ athlete-management engagement to come.

3. **The CBH advocacy ledger.** Margin's phone calls to college coaches are the credibility weapon of the entire CBH brand. The skill and data structure to log every call, track outcomes, and turn the log into the attribution ledger for future CBH sales is unique to this repo. By season's end, the advocacy ledger is the single most valuable sales asset Margin owns — second only to his actual record.
